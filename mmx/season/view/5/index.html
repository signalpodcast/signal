We Hold

Regular features include: News from the 'Verse, Listener Emails, What's Your Serenity?, The Tale of the Verse, Music Section, Badger's World, Bold Moves, Sci-Fi Review, Big Damn Test Kitchen and the Speculation Segment This season also includes many special features, and interviews with Scott Allie (Dark Horse Comics editor), Beth Nelson (Can't Stop the Serenity coordinator), Andy Gore (QMx), Jason Palmer (Serenity prints artist), Jack Ward and Shannon Hilchie (Sonic Society), Steven Brust (author, My Kind of Freedom) and Marian Call (musician, Got to Fly). Segment timings provided by Erik Hesse (aka Ironbandit).
