Niska's Necktie: The necktie knot we see Niska wearing is called an Onassis knot, after Aristotle Onassis (Jackie Kennedy Onassis's second husband) who used to wear this knot to public occasions. It is basically a full Windsor, just leaving off the last step of pulling the flap through, so that the broad end of the tie is hanging over the knot and therefore covering it.
