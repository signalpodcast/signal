Shadow (revisited)

Download audio file
First aired: The Signal: Season 3, Episode 6
Today we return to Shadow, and find it a far different world than the one we visited three years ago. The recent attack by Alliance forces has destroyed Shadow's Independent army completely, and the world has been left virtually uninhabitable. Incredibly, despite the assault, the Independent forces never surrendered here. The few survivors are now being evacuated to refugee camps around the 'Verse. This is a humanitarian mission, and we're here to assist.
