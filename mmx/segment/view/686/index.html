First aired: The Signal: Season 2, Episode 19
Four months ago, the Alliance began to enforce the declaration of Unification. Many of the Border and Rim worlds were happy to support Unification. Others, like Shadow, have declared open hostility toward the elected Parliament, with many of its young people volunteering to fight for independence. But most of the Border worlds are simply caught in the middle. How has the war affected them? We're here to find out.
