There are many good reasons why the war for Unification is being fought. We talk glibly about bringing civilization to the barbaric outer worlds, without ever really stopping to consider what that means. In this report, I highlight one of the most compelling reasons for unification: the buying and selling, of human beings. Today, I talk with one woman, whose business I would really like to see shut down.
