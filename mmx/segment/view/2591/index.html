Life in the 'Verse Special: Remembering Serenity Valley

First aired: The Signal: Season 7, Episode 21
A special broadcast of Life in the 'Verse looking at Serenity Valley through the memories of those who lived it. Life in the 'Verse is broadcast from Liann Jiun every Saturday at 18:00 Standard Time and is re-broadcast from local stations across the 'verse.
