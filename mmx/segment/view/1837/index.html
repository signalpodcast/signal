QMx

First aired: The Signal: Season 6, Episode 13
Les & Kari talk with Andy Gore, Karl Derrick & John Eblan of Quantum Mechanics about recent and upcoming product releases. Karl Derrick operates a Makeup, Monster Prop & Model studio in the UK; he's also is one of the world's leading Firefly prop collectors (he owns Vera), and is working with QMx on several new exciting products. John Eblan runs the model studio where all of the Serenity Big Damn Replicas and many more wonderful products are being created.
