Dark Places

Download audio file
Episode two of the now-abandoned series "Dark Places". Only two episodes were ever made.

Sound effects were provided by Digital Juice. The script editor was Carolyn Parkinson.

This episode (and the novel) contains sex, violence and swearing.
Definitely not safe for work!
