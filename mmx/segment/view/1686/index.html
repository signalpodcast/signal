A Review of Serenity Adventures

First aired: The Signal: Season 6, Episode 7
"Adventures" is a collection of modules for the Serenity Role Playing Game, from Margaret Weis publications, written by Alana Abbott, Billy Aguiar, James Davenport, Ted Reed and James M Ward, and edited by Jamie Chambers and Margaret Weis. Jill Arroway gives her take.
