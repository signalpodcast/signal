Borderline

First series • Previous series • Up • Next series • Last series
Borderline is a series of five-minute audio drama documentaries set in the universe of Joss Whedon's Firefly, documenting life on some of the Border and Rim worlds through the years of the Unification War ... from an Alliance point of view

Borderline is brought to you by Yoshida-Kendall Captures. Apparently. Well, actually, it's brought to you by the people who make The Signal - but don't tell the Alliance!

You can download these mp3 files individually, but for your convenience we have also archived them into podcast feeds. Right-click on an RSS icon, copy the link target/location, then paste it into your podcatching client. Alternatively, you can choose your own download interval and starting point to make your own custom feed.
